About us

We have been dedicated to printing since 1993. We provide a comprehensive service from graphic design through printing, storage and distribution (or installation).

2018, we have expanded our services to include: digital textile printing.

Got something you want to have printed? We're sure we can come up with something. We've already provided millions of prints, lots of prints of promotional items and textiles.

We rely on a personal approach and the certainty that everything will be done on time and at a high quality.
